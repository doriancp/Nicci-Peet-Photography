About

Nicci is a freelance photographer and writer based in Bristol.

Her passion for photographing drinks & food has led her to work with prestigious clients both nationally and internationally.

Published in: in October, Good Beer Hunting, Pellicle, Crumbs, Hop & Barley, Class Magazine, Froth Magazine, Ferment Magazine, Parallel Magazine, Bristol 24/7, Signature Chefs, Craft Beer Calendar NZ, Women in Art 278 Magazine, Fermenting Feminism, Bristol Women’s Voice, That’s What She Said and Bristol Woman

Previous Clients: Monkey Shoulder, Deya Brewery, Lost & Grounded, Cloudwater Brew Co., American Brewer's Association, We Are Beer, The Queer Brewing Project, Women On Tap, UK Brewery Tours, Yeastie Boys, Wild Beer Co., Wapping Wharf, Action Against Hunger, Food & Drink Guides, Foozie, NSPCC

Exhibitions:
In Progress, Women in Beer Portraits, Lost & Grounded Brewery, November 2018

Hop Art Show, Group Exhibition, Unit 101, Manchester, June/July 2018

Bristol Beer Week, Christmas Steps Gallery, Bristol, 2017

Caught on the Hop, Hop Hideout, Sheffield, 2015

Graduated with a BA in Photojournalism from London College of Communication
